Visiting San Diego

A travel guide from an ex-local

Downtown San Diego at night.

By Rufustelestrat (Own work) [GFDL, CC-BY-SA-3.0 or CC BY 2.5], via Wikimedia Commons

Hi! Whether you've already decided to visit San Diego, or you're just considering it, you've come to the right place. I grew up there, and even though I live in Toronto now, I still consider it home. This site is a collection of all the different bits of advice I've given over the years to friends going to visit, as well as all the stuff I like to make sure I do every time I head back. If there's something I've missed or something you'd like to hear more about, please drop me a line!

Why visit San Diego?

Sure, there are lots of great places to visit in the US and Canada, places that are a lot closer than San Diego. So why visit, other than the weather? Well, do you like beaches? Tacos? Mountains? Deserts? Surfers? Museums? Theater? Animals? Beer? Wine? All of those things, plus an average temperature of 21C, amazing scenery, great seafood, why aren't you booking a flight already?
